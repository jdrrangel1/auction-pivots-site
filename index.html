How It Works

Four numbers tell you where you are.

Every Auction Pivots study builds off the same weekly framework — no repainting, no discretionary guesswork about "the level."

01

Weekly Pivot

Calculated fresh each week from the prior week's range — the center of gravity price rotates around.

02

Extension Bands

Three tiers above and below the pivot band (X1, X2, X3) mark where price historically stalls or accelerates.

03

Open Classification

The moment a session opens, it's auto-tagged against the band stack — ABOVE_X2H, BELOW_WPL, and so on.

04

Mean-Reversion Tracking

See whether price has already touched back to the pivot this week, and when — before you take the next trade.
